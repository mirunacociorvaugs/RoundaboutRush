How to Play

Controls:

  • Press LEFT arrow key to move to outer lane
  • Press RIGHT arrow key to move to inner lane
  • On mobile: Tap the left/right buttons

Objective:

Navigate around the Max Orbit and avoid obstacles. Your Nordic Spirit Max can automatically orbits around the centre. Switch between the three rings to avoid the obstacles, claim power-ups and survive for as long as possible!

Scoring:

  • Earn points for each obstacle you pass
  • The game gets faster as your level increases
  • Collect powerups for special abilities

Power-Ups:

  • Slow Time: Slows down the game speed
  • Shield: Grants temporary invincibility
